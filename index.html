The Club
Hoosier Games aims to serve as the premier game development community of Bloomington, Indiana, bringing together people of all backgrounds, interests, and identities to form a supportive and productive learning environment.

Members of all skill levels are able to work, share ideas, and learn new techniques in an open collaborative environment. Additionally, individuals can form or join teams cenetered around either personal development and portfolio building or the creation of a full game.
