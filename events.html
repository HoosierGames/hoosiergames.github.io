Mid-Semester Standups
March 1 | 7:30pm | SB015
Come out and see the projects made and being made by Hoosier Games members during the first half of the semester! Production Teams will be showcasing their progress along their semester long journey to a full game product, and Specialist Teams will be presenting the results of their four-week projects.
Second Four-Week Pitches
March 8 | 7:30pm | SB015
Come here the ideas for the second round of portfolio-building-focused 4-week projects! These projects are open to anyone that wants to get a bit more experience working in their field of choice and are great for building up a strong collection of showcase-ready material!
GDC Week Game Night
March 22 | 7:30pm | SB015
With video games, board games, card games, and more, come out to a Hoosier Games Game Night to hang out with other members and have some free food and drinks. The space is open to all, so feel free to bring some friends!
Final Presentations
April 26 | 7:30pm | SB015
The last event of the semester, teams will be showing off their final products be it a game, collection of art pieces, soundtrack, or design prototype. Be sure to come out and see what everyone's been up to, talk with groups about their projects, and see what's in store for the future of Hoosier Games.
